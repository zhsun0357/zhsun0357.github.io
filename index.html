About

Hi, I am Jian (James) Wang, currently a Senior Research Scientist in Snap Inc. I obtained my PhD degree from Carnegie Mellon University ECE department, advised by Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan and Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan. I'm mainly interested in Computer Vision, Computational Imaging and Computational Photography.
I'm managed by Guru Krishnan and Shree K. Nayar in NYC research lab in Snap. Shree gave a nice keynote about our (unpublished) work in ICCP 2022 which includes my works, see here video link.
